# Learning-Coefficients
This repository collects papers and notes on local learning coefficients, including their theoretical foundations and applications. It is currently based primarily on the papers and book listed below, and references therein:
* Watanabe, Sumio. Algebraic geometry and statistical learning theory. Vol. 25. Cambridge university press, 2009.
* Deep learning is singular, and that’s good (https://arxiv.org/pdf/2010.11560) 
* Loss landscape geometry reveals stagewise development of transformers (https://openreview.net/pdf?id=2JabyZjM5H)
* Differentiation and specialization of attention heads via the refined local learning coefficient (https://proceedings.iclr.cc/paper_files/paper/2025/file/fad7c708dda11f3e72cc1629bb130379-Paper-Conference.pdf)
* Stagewise Reinforcement Learning and the Geometry of the Regret Landscape (https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.07524)
* Patterning: The Dual of Interpretability (https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.13548)
* Susceptibilities and Patterning: A Primer on Linear Response in Bayesian Learning (https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.07980)

I will continue updating this repository with my emerging ideas, mathematical proofs, notes, and relevant papers related to this topic.
